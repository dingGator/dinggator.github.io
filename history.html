The Legend of the Devil’s Millhopper

Once upon a time, a beautiful Indian Princess lived in a village near the present day Devils Millhopper sink hole. The Devil wanted to marry the Indian princess, but she did not want to marry the Devil. So the Devil kidnaps the princess and ran. All the Indian braves of her village were deeply saddened and began chasing the Devil and the princess. As the braves came closer to the Devil, the Devil created a huge sinkhole for the braves to fall into, thus the Devils Millhopper. When the braves tried to climb out of the sinkhole to save the princess, the Devil turned them to stone. Thus the weeping of water from the stones along the slopes of Devil’s Millhopper are the tears of the Indian braves shed for the Indian princess.
